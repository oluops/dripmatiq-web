# Summer Micro-Capsule: UPF + Cute, Not Boring

Summer means sun, heat, unpredictable plans. You can still look cute, avoid sunburn, and travel light.

This 7-piece micro-capsule focuses on UPF protection plus mix-and-match pieces that keep outfits interesting. Goal: 14 unique looks from 7 items, one outfit per day for a 10-day trip with smart rewears.

## Why UPF matters

UPF fabrics block harmful UV rays. If you'll be outdoors for more than an hour, UPF pieces reduce sun damage risk and remove need for dramatic outfit swaps when sun intensity changes.

## The 7-piece capsule

- white UPF tee or sun-protective button-up
- lightweight midi skirt (neutral)
- breathable linen shorts (neutral)
- cotton-linen layer (lightweight) for evenings
- neutral sandals
- compact packable hat or scarf
- one statement top or accessory (pattern or color)

## Packing tips

- Roll items to save space
- Use packing cubes for clean separation
- Plan two pairs of shoes for heavy walking days

## Rewear strategy

- Wash small items in sink overnight
- Swap accessories to change vibe
- Use layer + hat to refresh repeated tops

## 7-day edit for city travel

- Day 1: Arrival casual + hat
- Day 2: Sightseeing  UPF tee + shorts
- Day 3: Dinner out  skirt + statement top
- Day 4: Museum + layer
- Day 5: Beach morning + hat
- Day 6: City stroll + sandals
- Day 7: Travel day  neutral, easy pack

## Why this capsule works

Simple neutral base, one statement, targeted UPF protection. Travel-friendly, mixable, low decision fatigue.
