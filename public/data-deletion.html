Data Deletion

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This page explains how Dripmatiq users can delete their data and account.

Option 1: Delete data directly in the app (instant self-service)

  1. Open Dripmatiq on your device.
  2. Go to the Style tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom.
  4. Tap Delete All Data and confirm.

This removes your wardrobe items, saved outfits, generated images, and related preferences from your account.

Option 2: Request account deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email support@dripmatiq.app from the address associated with your account and use subject line: Account Deletion Request.

What is deleted

  • Account profile and authentication record
  • Wardrobe metadata and garment photos
  • Saved outfits and outfit history
  • AI-generated outfit images tied to your account

Processing time

In-app deletion is processed immediately once confirmed. Email requests are processed as quickly as possible, typically within 7 days.
