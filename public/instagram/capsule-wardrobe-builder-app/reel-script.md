**Audio:** Voiceover + background music

## PRODUCTION NOTES
- **Music:** Low-fi, confident, mid-tempo (90-100 BPM). Instrumental.
- **Voiceover:** Warm, conversational, slightly faster than normal speech (~160 wpm).
- **Typography:** Georgia serif for headlines, Arial for body. Gold (#D4A847) for emphasis.
- **Transitions:** Hard cuts between frames, 0.3s cross-dissolve on text within frames.
- **Safe Zones:** Keep all critical text within center 80% (avoid Reels UI overlay zones).
