I built a capsule wardrobe twice. Both times it failed.

First time: 37-piece rule, donated 2 bags. Still wore the same 6 outfits.
Second time: 30-piece challenge, photographed the grid. Still froze at 7 AM for a client call.

The problem wasn't the clothes. It was the OCCASIONS.

Magazine capsules are for someone else's life — coffee meetings, gallery openings, dinner parties. My real week: WFH Mon-Wed, gym Thu AM, client visit Thu PM, casual weekends, one weird formal obligation.

No 37-piece universal list covers that without dead weight.

The shift: Stop asking "Does this spark joy?" Start asking "Which occasion does this cover?"

If a piece only works for 1 occasion twice a year → not earning its space.
If it stretches across 3 weekly contexts → it's doing real work.
